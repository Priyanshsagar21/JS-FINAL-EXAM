TVS RAIDER

TVS Raider: The Smart Commuter for the Youth The TVS Raider is a dynamic 125cc commuter motorcycle from TVS Motor Company, crafted for young riders who value style, performance, and practicality. Its sporty design, cutting-edge features, and efficient performance set it apart in the segment. Key Highlights:

Engine & Performance: Powered by a 124.8cc single-cylinder air- and oil-cooled engine, it delivers 11.2 hp and 11.2 Nm torque, paired with a smooth 5-speed gearbox. The Raider is built for spirited city commutes and occasional highway rides.

Sporty Design: With a sharp headlamp, bold graphics, and a sculpted fuel tank, the Raider exudes a premium and aggressive look. The split-seat design ensures both comfort and style.

Advanced Features: Comes equipped with dual Ride Modes (Eco and Power), a fully digital instrument cluster displaying speed, gear position, fuel efficiency, and real-time mileage. It also features a USB charging port for convenience.

Comfort & Safety: A telescopic front suspension and mono-shock rear suspension provide superior ride quality. Braking is enhanced with a front disc and rear drum option with synchronized braking technology (SBT).

Fuel Efficiency: Delivers an impressive 60-70 km/l, making it both economical and practical.

TVS Apache: Power Meets Precision

The TVS Apache series, a flagship offering from TVS Motor Company, is designed for performance enthusiasts who crave speed, style, and cutting-edge technology. Known for its aggressive design and racing DNA, the Apache delivers a thrilling riding experience whether you're on city streets or a racetrack.

Key Features: Performance Engines: The Apache series is available in engine variants ranging from 160cc to 310cc, offering a range of power for different riding styles. Features like Race-Tuned Fuel Injection (RT-Fi) and Ride Modes deliver excellent throttle response and adaptability.

Aggressive Design: With sharp, aerodynamic styling, signature LED lights, and a sporty stance, the Apache makes a statement on the road. Its design is influenced by motorsport, giving it a bold and racing-inspired look.

Advanced Technology: Features include SmartXonnect, which connects the bike to your smartphone, offering turn-by-turn navigation, ride analytics, and more. The fully-digital instrument cluster, along with a slipper clutch, enhances both convenience and performance.

Racing Pedigree: Built from the TVS Racing legacy, the Apache series is engineered for performance with Race-Tuned Suspension and optimized aerodynamics for superior handling and stability.

Comfort and Safety: Equipped with a mono-shock rear suspension for better ride quality and dual-channel ABS for enhanced safety and control, especially during high-speed braking.

TVS Ronin: The Urban Cruiser

The TVS Ronin is a unique blend of modern styling, powerful performance, and advanced technology. Positioned as a versatile urban cruiser, the Ronin caters to those who desire a motorcycle that can effortlessly handle both daily commutes and long weekend rides.

Key Features:
Engine & Performance: The TVS Ronin is powered by a 225.9cc single-cylinder, air-oil cooled engine, delivering an output of 20.4 hp and 19.93 Nm of torque. This engine provides a perfect balance of power and efficiency, making it suitable for both city and highway riding.

Design & Styling: The Ronin stands out with its rugged yet stylish design, featuring a scrambler-inspired look with a muscular tank, LED lighting, and a unique headlamp design. The design blends modern aesthetics with retro cues, offering a distinctive presence on the road.

Advanced Technology: Equipped with a digital instrument cluster, the TVS Ronin offers real-time ride data, including speed, fuel efficiency, and trip information. It also features ride-by-wire technology for smoother throttle response and enhanced control.

Comfort & Handling: With a long-travel suspension system, the Ronin offers a comfortable ride, even on rough terrains. The combination of wide handlebars and a relaxed seating posture ensures that the rider experiences comfort during both short and long rides.

Safety Features: The TVS Ronin is equipped with dual-channel ABS, providing enhanced safety during sudden braking. The bike also features a high ground clearance and durable tyres for added stability and grip.
